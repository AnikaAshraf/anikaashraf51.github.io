Best Layered Cake

We are obsessed with the layered cake and it is as tasty as Lord Stow layered cake. I tried Lord Stow tart at Rua do Chuna shop. For me, I like Lord Stow shell because it is much softer on outter layer. Margerat tart egg custord is much richer. Depends on individual taste bud. Both are relatively good. But for Margeret's tart, you need to q-up. We q about 20 minutes when we arrive around 3pm. Second day morning, we arrive before their shop open at 10am and there are already people waiting.
